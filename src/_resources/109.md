description: The Database of Chinese Trial Cases (中國審判案例要覽), published by Renmin University
  of China Press in collaboration with the National Judicial College and the Renmin
  University Law School, provides access to selected cases from Chinese courts at
  all levels drawn from the China Trial Cases (中國審判案例要覽) series. The database includes
  guiding cases, typical cases, and gazette cases spanning three decades of judicial
  practice, and is one of two databases mandated by the Supreme People’s Court for
  similar-case research by Chinese judges.
