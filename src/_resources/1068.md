description: Taiwan Academic Classics (TAC) is an interdisciplinary digital research
  platform from United Digital Publications that brings together over 6,600 classical
  texts spanning China's earliest dynasties, approximately 300 academic journals in
  the humanities and social sciences, and more than 100,000 dissertations in Chinese
  studies. The platform integrates content from leading Taiwanese academic institutions,
  including journals and rare materials from Academia Sinica and the National Palace
  Museum, searchable across two billion characters. TAC also includes the punctuated
  full-text edition of the Gujin tushu jicheng and the Scripta Sinica journal database.
