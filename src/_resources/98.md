description: The Taiwan Wenxian Congkan Database (台灣文獻叢刊資料庫), hosted by the Institute
  of Taiwan History (Academia Sinica), provides full-text access to 311 texts from
  the Taiwan Wenxian Congkan (Taiwan Historical Documents Series), a corpus of works
  on Taiwanese geography, history, customs, and humanities published by the Taiwan
  Bank Economic Research Office between 1957 and 1972. The database contains approximately
  50 million characters and supports full-text search and chapter-level e-book browsing.
