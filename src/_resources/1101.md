  magazine founded in 1966 by novelist Louis Cha (Jin Yong). It has served as a major
  platform for Chinese intellectual discourse, publishing essays, criticism, creative
  writing, and commentary on mainland China, Hong Kong, and Taiwan. This electronic
  archive covers the magazine from 1966 to 2000, providing full-text access to over
  three decades of cultural and political commentary.
