description: The Kuo Ting-yee Library Digital Archive (郭廷以圖書館數位典藏系統), hosted by the
  Institute of Modern History (Academia Sinica), provides unified access to the library's
  digitized holdings, including newspapers, clippings, gazettes, periodicals, books,
  and archival documents. The system, relaunched in 2021, integrates previously scattered
  databases into a single searchable interface with 1.76 million metadata records
  and 4.39 million full-text image files as of December 2023.
