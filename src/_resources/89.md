description: The Research Platform for Women's Biographies (近代婦女傳記資料庫), hosted by
  the Institute of Modern History (Academia Sinica), compiles biographical records
  of women from the modern period with the aim of addressing the male-dominated bias
  in existing biographical databases. Each entry includes personal attributes (native
  place, birth and death dates, educational background) and tagged social relationships
  linking individuals to organizations and events, enabling social network analysis
  of women's communities in modern China.
