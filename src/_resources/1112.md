description: The CEIC China Premium Database is a leading macroeconomic and financial
  time series platform covering over 300,000 data series for China running back to
  1949. It covers national accounts, government and public finance, demographic and
  labor market statistics, inflation, foreign trade, foreign direct investment, financial
  markets, and industry-sector data. The database includes subnational data at provincial,
  municipal, and county levels for over 200 cities, as well as comparable data for
  Brazil, India, Indonesia, and Russia. Data can be exported in multiple formats and
  visualized through built-in charting tools.
