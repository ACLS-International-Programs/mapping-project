description: The Integrated Information System on Modern and Contemporary Characters
  (IISMCC / 近現代人物資訊整合系統), hosted by the Institute of Modern History (Academia Sinica),
  aggregates biographical information from multiple source types — biographical registers,
  official position records, archival finding aids, and oral history indices — covering
  individuals from the mid-Qing dynasty through the Republic of China era. The database
  currently includes over 135,000 individuals and links outward to related MHDB systems
  including the Modern Annals Timelines Information System.
