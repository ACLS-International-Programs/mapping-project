description: READCHINA was a research project led by Lena Henningsen at the University
  of Freiburg (2018–2023) investigating the politics of reading in the PRC, with a
  primary focus on the Cultural Revolution era. Its core output is ReadAct, an open-access
  relational database documenting over 1,000 individual reading acts drawn from autobiographical
  sources, recording what individuals read during the Cultural Revolution alongside
  metadata on agents, works, and spatial context. The dataset is openly available
  on GitHub; the project site also includes resources on hand-copied books (shouchaoben),
  comics, and scholarly publications.
