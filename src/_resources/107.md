description: The Ming Government Official Titles crowd-translation project, funded
  by the Andrew W. Mellon Foundation through the Council on East Asian Libraries (CEAL)
  Innovation Grant, produced a comprehensive Chinese-English bilingual dictionary
  of Ming dynasty government official titles, supplementing Charles Hucker’s Dictionary
  of Official Titles in Imperial China. The dictionary is freely available via UC
  eScholarship, and an API for Ming official titles continues to support the China
  Biographical Database (CBDB). The platform at UCI Libraries remains live and open
  for scholarly contributions.
