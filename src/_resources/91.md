description: MHGIS (Modern History Geographical Information Services / 近代歷史地理資訊平台)
  is a historical GIS platform jointly developed by the Institute of Modern History
  and the Center for GIS, RCHSS, Academia Sinica. The platform provides georectified
  historical maps with thematic coverage of treaty ports, concessions, customs offices,
  railway hubs, and major cities across East and Southeast Asia, with map types including
  large-scale city maps (1:25,000 and above), topographic maps, waterway charts, and
  railway maps. Map resources integrate with other MHDB databases including the Databases
  of Chinese Maritime Customs.
