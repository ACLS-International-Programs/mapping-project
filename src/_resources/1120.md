description: World Newsreels Online is a streaming video collection of international
  newsreels produced between 1929 and 1966, including footage from Japan, France,
  the United States, the Netherlands, and other countries. All items are accompanied
  by transcripts, with non-English content translated into English. The collection
  covers key events of the mid-twentieth century including World War II and the Second
  Sino-Japanese War (1937–1945), comprising nine distinct newsreel series — including
  France Actualités, Nippon News, and The March of Time — totaling over 500 hours
  of content.
