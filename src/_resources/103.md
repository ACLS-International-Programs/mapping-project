  Staatsbibliothek) provides open access to approximately 2 million images of Chinese,
  Japanese, and Korean printings and manuscripts held by the library, covering the
  7th through 19th centuries. The collection is particularly strong in Chinese materials
  and includes rare items spanning the Tang through Qing dynasties.
