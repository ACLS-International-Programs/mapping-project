description: The Modern Urban Tabloids Database, developed by the Urban History Research
  Group at the Institute of Modern History (Academia Sinica), compiles digitized images
  and bibliographic metadata from 13 urban tabloid newspapers published in Shanghai
  and Suzhou during the Republican era, comprising approximately 890,000 records.
  Opened for public use in 2015, the database supports research on urban social and
  cultural history, and includes material documenting civil life in Suzhou during
  the Japanese occupation.
