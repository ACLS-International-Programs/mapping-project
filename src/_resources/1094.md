description: 'Lawinfochina and Chinalawinfo are companion legal retrieval systems
  for the PRC: Lawinfochina provides the English interface and content; Chinalawinfo
  provides Chinese. Both are accessible from the same platform and link to each other.
  Included are: all laws adopted by the National People''s Congress and its Standing
  Committee from 1949; all administrative regulations promulgated by the State Council
  from 1949; rules and orders from other agencies; important judicial interpretations
  and regulations impacting foreign-related activities; judicial cases from the Gazette
  of the Supreme People''s Court (also in English); Sino-foreign tax treaties; and
  other legal news. The platform also includes a Chinese case studies database within
  Chinalawinfo.'
