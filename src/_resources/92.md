description: The Modern Transformation of Talented Women's Culture database (才女文化的現代轉型),
  a collaborative project between the Institute of Modern History (Academia Sinica)
  and McGill University, provides biographical records for over 800 talented women
  (cainü) from the late Qianlong period through the Republican era. Each entry documents
  personal attributes and tagged social relationships — family, literary, and social
  networks — drawing on classical compilations of women's writings. Relational data
  is available for download to support social network analysis.
