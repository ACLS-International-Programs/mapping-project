description: 'The Qing Dynasty Memorial Archives database, hosted by the Institute
  of Modern History (Academia Sinica), provides access to digitized Qing dynasty archival
  materials in two main categories: finance and taxation records, and criminal case
  records. Financial holdings include imperial rescripts, Board of Revenue memorials,
  salt and tax administration documents, and Imperial Household Department accounts;
  criminal holdings include the Board of Punishments memorial catalog and the Chinese
  Yellow Registers of the Grand Secretariat. The database offers broad geographic
  and thematic coverage across the full Qing period.'
