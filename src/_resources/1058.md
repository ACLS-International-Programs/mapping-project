description: 'A collaborative digitization project, sponsored by Chiang Ching-kuo
  Foundation for International Scholarly Exchange Program, which includes Chinese
  rare books digitized from the collections of four participating libraries: the Asian
  Division of the Library of Congress, the East Asian Library and the Gest Collection
  of Princeton University, the Harvard-Yenching Library of Harvard University, and
  the Fu Ssu-Nien Library of the Institute of History and Philology of the Academia
  Sinica in Taiwan. Each library provided some 100,000 images, covering between 171
  and 295 titles each.  They have now been made available free of charge to the world.
  A description of the project is available.'
