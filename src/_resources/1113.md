description: The National Bureau of Statistics of China (NBS) website provides free
  access to official Chinese government statistical data, including monthly, quarterly,
  and annual series on national accounts, population, employment, prices, trade, and
  industry. The site hosts the full run of China Statistical Yearbooks and population
  census data, with regional breakdowns by province and municipality. Data are available
  in both Chinese and English. The NBS is the primary authoritative source for official
  PRC macroeconomic and social statistics.
