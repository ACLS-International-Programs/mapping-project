description: Duxiu (读秀) is a large-scale discovery and full-text database for Chinese-language
  academic content, comparable in scope to a combined Google Scholar and Google Books
  but with features unique to the Chinese academic publishing ecosystem. The database
  contains over 600 million full-text pages, with flexible search across books, journal
  articles, theses, web pages, and newspapers. Duxiu works best with Firefox; users
  may encounter problems when requesting PDFs using Chrome.
