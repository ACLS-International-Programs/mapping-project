description: The Old Hong Kong Newspapers collection, maintained by Hong Kong Public
  Libraries and accessible through their Digital Collection platform, provides digitized
  images of 14 major historical Hong Kong newspapers spanning from 1853 to 1991. Chinese-language
  titles include 香港工商日報 (1926–1984), 華僑日報 (1947–1991), 香港華字日報 (1895–1940), 大公報 (1938–1991),
  遐邇貫珍 (1853–1856), and 循環日報 (1874–1886); English-language titles include the China
  Mail (1866–1961), Hong Kong Daily Press (1864–1941), and Hong Kong Telegraph (1881–1951),
  among others. The collection supports research into Hong Kong's historical development,
  society, and culture from the colonial era through the late twentieth century.
