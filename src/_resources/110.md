description: The Zhongguo Lidai Muzhi Shujuku (中國歷代墓誌數據庫), developed by Zhejiang University
  Library, is a freely accessible database of epitaph inscriptions spanning Chinese
  history from antiquity through the Qing dynasty. The collection contains approximately
  6,800 records with particular strength in Tang dynasty epitaphs; most entries include
  scanned rubbing images that can be browsed and downloaded. The database supports
  research in prosopography, historical geography, and epigraphy.
