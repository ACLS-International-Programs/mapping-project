description: The China Visual Arts Project Archive, held at the University of Westminster,
  is a collection of over 800 Chinese political propaganda posters acquired since
  the 1970s, spanning the 1940s through the 1980s. The collection covers the late
  Republican era, the Maoist period, and the early Reform era, and includes accompanying
  books, objects, and ephemera. Digitized versions of the posters are accessible through
  the University of Westminster Archive catalogue.
