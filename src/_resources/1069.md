description: Set of 5,328 full-text searchable classical Chinese titles forming an
  important addition to the Siku quanshu. Based on an assessment of important works
  post-dating the 18th-century Siku compilation, it is arranged in the same classification
  scheme and is strongest in Qing material, but also includes better versions of some
  earlier texts. Originally published in 1,800 volumes drawn from over 80 Chinese
  libraries. The database allows comparison of OCR text with original page images.
  Available as part of the Diaolong database collection; note that the Xuxiu Siku
  quanshu content appears as 'Siku fenlei shu 四庫分類書' in the Fanyun wenxian 繃雲文獻 alternative
  portal.
