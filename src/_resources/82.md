description: Scripta Sinica is one of the largest Chinese full-text databases of traditional
  classical texts, developed by the Institute of History and Philology (Academia Sinica)
  beginning in 1984. It currently encompasses over 1,506 titles totaling more than
  833 million characters, spanning all four branches of the traditional canon (jing,
  shi, zi, ji) with particular depth in historical materials. The database supports
  full-text keyword search across the entire corpus.
