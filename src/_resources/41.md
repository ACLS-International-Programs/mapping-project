description: China Global Development Dashboard (formerly China.AidData.org) tracks
  China's global overseas development finance through systematic research by AidData,
  a research lab at William & Mary. The platform documents over 31,750 projects totaling
  more than $2.17 trillion in tracked commitments across Africa, Asia, Latin America,
  and other regions, covering loans, grants, technical assistance, and other forms
  of development cooperation. Data is downloadable and accompanied by interactive
  visualizations and analytical reports, enabling researchers, policymakers, and journalists
  to study patterns in Chinese development finance and assess the scope of China's
  global development activities.
