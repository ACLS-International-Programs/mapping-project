description: The Chinese Engineers Relational Database (CERD), developed by Pelzer
  et al. (2021–2023) and hosted by the Digital Humanities Initiative at the Hong Kong
  University of Science and Technology, is a relational database of engineers from
  the Chinese Republican period (1912–1949). The current public dataset includes 17,618
  engineers with linked records for 663 colleges, 17,461 degrees, 9,245 workplaces,
  and 25,475 occupations.
