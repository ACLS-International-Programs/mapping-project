description: The Firms in Modern China Database (近代商號資料庫), developed by the Urban
  History Research Group at the Institute of Modern History (Academia Sinica), compiles
  structured records on commercial firms in Republican-era cities including Beijing,
  Tianjin, Shanghai, Suzhou, and Wuhan, drawn from historical city guides. Each record
  includes the firm's city, address, period, industry classification, business activities,
  scale, and names and basic information of managers, supporting research on commercial
  networks and business history in modern China.
