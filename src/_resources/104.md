description: The Biographical Dictionary of Chinese Christianity (BDCC) is an electronic
  database of biographical records covering Chinese and foreign Christian missionaries,
  church leaders, evangelists, and laity who shaped Chinese Christian communities
  from approximately 1800 to 1950. With particular focus on lesser-known Chinese Christians
  of the modern era, the project is produced by an international team of contributors
  from academic, church, and mission communities across China, Asia, and the West.
