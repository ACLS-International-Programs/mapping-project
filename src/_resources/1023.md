description: 'A text-searchable database of ca. 7,000 Chinese periodicals published
  from late Qing to 1949 in all fields of knowledge: the humanities, social sciences,
  exact sciences, as well as popular journals. Examples include the Shidianzhai huabao,
  Xiandai, Shaonian Zhongguo, Dazhong shenghuo, Guocui xuebao, Qingnian Zhongguo,
  Minfeng. Listings per subject area are provided. Contents are regularly updated.
  One can search by keyword in title in simplified or traditional Chinese, which retrieves
  article titles; clicking on such a hit retrieves the page with the term, which however
  is not highlighted. One can browse by category of publication (use the pop-up menu
  called +fenlei tixi). Clicking on a periodical title gives the issues available
  of that title; clicking on the issue gives a table of content. PDFs can be downloaded.
  Advanced searching (by article or periodical title) is available. This database
  complements the Quanguo baokan suoyin database.'
