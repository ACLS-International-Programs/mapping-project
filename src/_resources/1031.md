  with the National Security Archive at George Washington University, is a comprehensive
  collection of declassified U.S. government primary documents on U.S. foreign and
  military policy since 1945. The database includes over 750,000 documents organized
  into more than 40 topic-based collections. East Asian modules include: China: US
  Intelligence and China: Collection, Analysis, and Covert Action, 1945–2010; China
  and the United States: From Hostility to Engagement, 1960–1998. Japan: Japan and
  the United States—Diplomatic, Security, and Economic Relations, Parts I, II, and
  III (1960–2000). Korea: The United States and The Two Koreas, Parts I and II (1969–2010).'
