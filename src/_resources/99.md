description: The Modern Women Journals Database (近代婦女期刊資料庫), hosted by the Institute
  of Modern History (Academia Sinica), provides bibliographic records and original
  image access for 213 women's and gender-related periodicals from the Republican
  era, comprising approximately 110,000 items. Opened for public use in 2015, the
  database supports research in women's history, periodical history, and the publishing
  industry in Republican China, and integrates with the Integrated Information System
  on Modern and Contemporary Characters via author records.
