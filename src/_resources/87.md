description: The Qing Dynasty Official Positions Database (清代職官資料庫), developed by
  the Institute of History and Philology (Academia Sinica) beginning in 2007, documents
  the official structure of Qing central and local government. Based on the five-dynasty
  Huidian, the Qing History Draft, and primary archival sources including Grand Secretariat
  archives and palace memorials, the database covers institutional history, official
  ranks and responsibilities, administrative jurisdictions, and annual rosters of
  officeholders. Users can link directly to the biographical database and historical
  place name system within the IHP system.
