description: The Modern Annals Timelines Information System (TIS / 近代春秋TIS系統), developed
  by the Institute of Modern History (Academia Sinica), is a timeline-based database
  covering two centuries of modern Chinese history. Primary sources include the Daily
  Records of the History of the Republic of China and the Detailed Preliminary Draft
  of Major Events in the Life of Chiang Kai-shek, supplemented by diaries of prominent
  modern figures including Hsu Yung-chang and Wang Shih-chieh.
