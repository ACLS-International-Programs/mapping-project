description: Searchable index of articles on China appearing in Japanese academic
  journals, including many less commonly-indexed periodicals. Published by Nichigai
  Associates, the database covers a broad range of disciplines and includes reduced-image
  copies of the indexed articles. The printed multi-volume collection Chugoku kankei
  ronsetsu shiryō sakuin (中国関係論説資料索引) has been published since the 1970s and is a
  standard reference tool for Japan-based China scholarship.
