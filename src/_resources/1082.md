description: Open Books Hong Kong is an open access initiative of the university presses
  of The Chinese University of Hong Kong, City University of Hong Kong, and The University
  of Hong Kong. A selection of peer-reviewed scholarly books published by these three
  university presses is made freely available to the public. Works focus on humanities
  and social sciences, primarily in Chinese, authored by Hong Kong and international
  scholars. New titles are released quarterly. All titles are licensed under CC BY-NC-ND
  4.0.
