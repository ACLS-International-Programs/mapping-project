description: The China Data Lab at UC San Diego constructs and indexes datasets for
  social scientific research on China, focusing on text data from Chinese media, social
  media, and legal documents; biographical data on the Chinese elite; public opinion
  surveys; and firm-level data related to China's outbound investment. The lab also
  collects and indexes important public datasets used by China researchers covering
  health, environment, GIS, law, media, and religion, organized in a searchable link
  library.
