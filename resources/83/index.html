    Modern Urban Tabloids Database

    Alternate Title
    近代城市小報資料庫
    Category
    Newspapers
    Institution
    Institute of Modern History, Academia Sinica
    Access
    open access
    URL
    https://mhdb.mh.sinica.edu.tw/newsp/
    Description
    The Modern Urban Tabloids Database, developed by the Urban History Research Group at the Institute of Modern History (Academia Sinica), compiles digitized images and bibliographic metadata from 13 urban tabloid newspapers published in Shanghai and Suzhou during the Republican era, comprising approximately 890,000 records. Opened for public use in 2015, the database supports research on urban social and cultural history, and includes material documenting civil life in Suzhou during the Japanese occupation.
